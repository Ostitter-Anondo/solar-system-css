A Scaled Model of the Solar System Made Using CSS Animations

Calculation Methodology for The Orbit

Here I used a scaled vesion of orbit times. Following is the method used for calculating the orbit times.

Assuming the orbit time for earth as 1, and scaling the orbit period in a 1 year:5 seconds ratio, the following time can be calculated.

Planet Scale of 1 Scale of 5s
Mercury 0.241 1.2 seconds
Venus 0.615 3.1 seconds
Earth 1 5 seconds
Mars 1.881 9.41 seconds
Jupiter 11.86 59.3 seconds
Saturn 29.46 147.3 seconds
Uranus 84.01 420.1 seconds
Neptune 164.8 824 seconds
